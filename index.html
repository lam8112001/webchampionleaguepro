The new brand identity introduces vibrant, contemporary design principles that signal a bold step forward for club football’s most prestigious competition.

HIGHLIGHTING THE MOMENTS
THAT MAKE THE ULTIMATE STAGE

Crafted from light, the starball illuminates the spectacular moments that transcend sport. It scales to frame the details that matter, from a pulsating stadium to the artful flick of a player’s ankle.

The starball moves in harmony with football’s most thrilling performances, capturing and immortalising the action.
